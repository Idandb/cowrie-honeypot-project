KB001: VPN Client Setup Guide (Windows 10/11)
---------------------------------------------
1. Download the CompanyVPN client from http://intranet.companyserver.com/downloads/CompanyVPN_Client_latest.msi
2. Run the installer with administrator privileges.
3. After installation, launch CompanyVPN client.
4. Enter server address: vpn.companyserver.com
5. Authenticate with your CompanyServer username and password.
6. If connection fails, verify your internet connection and ensure port 443 UDP is not blocked by your firewall.
7. For persistent issues, collect client logs and open a ticket with IT Support.
